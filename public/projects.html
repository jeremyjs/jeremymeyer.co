Check out some projects I've been working on!

LiveCourse

LiveCourse allows Purdue students to chat and collaboratively take notes in all of their favorite classes cleanly and easily. We built it with Backbone.js and CodeIgniter, which uses MVC. Clicking the title or picture will take you to the homepage, but you would have to create an account to be able to access the site itself. We also developed applications for Android and Windows Phone.

PurdueMade

I'm working on PurdueMade with some other Purdue students for the Entrepreneurship and Innovation Club. We're building PurdueMade with Meteor, but it's still a work in progress. We're rolling it out in the fall at the start of the school year. Clicking the title or picture will take you to the Github repository.
