"You can make a difference." Everbody says that. But we've become immune to cries for help. In this busy, busy world of ours, we stop caring about problems because our eyes just glaze over when the 50th Facebook post with some sad story rolls by on our news feed. But the thing is, we never actually do anything about it. Instead of stopping to think about which charity deserves our attention, we ignore them all.

As you can see from the video, getting clean water is a real problem. Charity Water is the solution. If you think this is a cause worth supporting, click this link.

http://my.charitywater.org/timferriss

That's the page of Tim Ferriss, a famous entrepreneur and blogger. He decided to use his 36th birthday as an opportunity to make a real impact. You can be part of that impact. If you want to offer a hand up and not a hand out, clean water can have an amazing effect for breaking the cycle of poverty. Every $1 invested in improved water access and sanitation can yield an average of $12 in economic returns. Whether it's $1, $36, or a million, your donation counts.
