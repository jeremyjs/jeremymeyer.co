Purdue Entrepreneurship and Innovation Club

The Purdue Entrepreneurship and Innovation Club is leading the effort to create better opportunities for student entrepreneurs at Purdue to get from ideation to acquisition and beyond. I have served on the executive board since January 2013. Click on the picture or title to go to the homepage. I am currently on the development team for PurdueMade, a website to provide networking and resource opportunities to Purdue students and alumni. PurdueMade will be going live at the start of the school year.

BoilerMake Hackathon

I am on the executive board for BoilerMake, a hackathon at Purdue on October 11-13. This is the first year a hackathon of this magnitude (400+ participants) has been held at Purdue, and we expect it to continue for years to come. Clicking on the title or picture will take you to the homepage. Check out the Facebook page here.

Purdue Night Train

As the premier swing dance club at Purdue University, Purdue Night Train has over 100 members and counting. I have served on the executive board as the Web Designer since April 2013, and have since done a major update on the website.
